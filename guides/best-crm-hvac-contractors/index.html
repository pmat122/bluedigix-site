How to Choose the Right CRM for Your HVAC Business

The "best" CRM depends on your specific situation:

  • Solo contractor or small team (1-3 techs): Start with Jobber or Housecall Pro. Graduate to GoHighLevel when you're ready to focus on growth and marketing.
  • Growth-focused contractor (3-10 techs): GoHighLevel is the clear winner. The marketing automation and lead conversion features will drive more revenue than any field service feature.
  • Large operation (10+ techs): Consider ServiceTitan for dispatch, but run GoHighLevel alongside it for marketing. Many successful contractors use both.

The real question: What's your bottleneck? If it's dispatch and scheduling, you need ServiceTitan or Housecall Pro. If it's lead conversion and follow-up (which is most contractors), you need GoHighLevel.
