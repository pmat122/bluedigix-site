The Solution: Instagram DM Automation

How Comment Trigger Automation Works

Step 1: The Trigger

You post a before/after photo of a bathroom remodel. In your caption: "Want a free quote? Comment QUOTE below."

Step 2: The Auto-DM

Someone comments "QUOTE" on your post. Within seconds, ManyChat automatically sends them a DM:

"Hey! Thanks for reaching out about a quote. Quick question—what type of project are you looking at?"

[Kitchen] [Bathroom] [Full Remodel] [Other]

Step 3: The Intake Flow

They tap "Bathroom." ManyChat asks qualifying questions: zip code, timeline, best number to reach them.

Step 4: Lead Captured in Your CRM

All that info gets automatically pushed into your CRM. By the time you climb down from that ladder, you've got a fully qualified lead—name, number, project type, and timeline.
