Automation Workflows Every Pest Control Company Needs

1. New Lead Follow-Up (Speed-to-Lead)

  1. Instant (0 min): Text + email confirming you received their inquiry
  2. 5 minutes: Phone call attempt from your team
  3. 1 hour: If no answer, follow-up text with booking link
  4. 24 hours: Email with customer testimonials
  5. 72 hours: "Still need help with pests?" final follow-up

2. Service Reminder Sequence

  1. 7 days before: "Your quarterly treatment is coming up" + confirmation link
  2. 1 day before: "We'll be there tomorrow between [time]" + technician info
  3. Day of: "Your technician is on the way" with live ETA

3. Post-Service Review Request

  1. Same day: "How was your service today?" (rating request)
  2. If 4-5 stars: Direct link to Google review
  3. If 1-3 stars: Alert to manager for follow-up call

4. Contract Renewal Campaign

  1. 60 days before expiration: "Your annual plan is coming up for renewal"
  2. 30 days: "Renew now and lock in your current rate"
  3. 14 days: "Don't let your protection lapse" + discount offer
  4. 7 days: Final warning + phone call from account manager
