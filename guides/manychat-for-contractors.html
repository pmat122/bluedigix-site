How Comment Trigger Automation Works

Let's break down the exact flow we set up for our contractor clients:

Step 1: The Trigger

You post a before/after photo of a bathroom remodel. In your caption, you write: "Want a free quote? Comment QUOTE below."

Step 2: The Auto-DM

Someone comments "QUOTE" on your post. Within seconds, ManyChat automatically sends them a DM:

"Hey! Thanks for reaching out about a quote. I'd love to help. Quick question—what type of project are you looking at?"

[Kitchen] [Bathroom] [Full Remodel] [Other]

Step 3: The Intake Flow

They tap "Bathroom." ManyChat asks a few more qualifying questions:

  • What's your zip code?
  • What's your timeline?
  • What's the best number to reach you?

Step 4: Lead Captured in Your CRM

All that info gets automatically pushed into your CRM. We recommend GoHighLevel because it integrates seamlessly with ManyChat and lets you automate lead follow-up with texts and emails.

By the time you climb down from that ladder, you've got a fully qualified lead sitting in your pipeline—name, number, project type, and timeline. All you have to do is call them back.
